How to play

To make a flower arrangement, you first have to select a flower to place.
You can do this by clicking on the flower's name in your inventory (on the left).

Once you place a flower, that tile will show your flower name, and a number, like so: "Daisy (4)"
The number is the score that flower gives you from being in the arrangement.

When you're satisfied with your arrangement, click "submit arrangement".
You will recieve money based on how high your arrangement score was.
In addition, every once in a while you can restock your flowers from the shop.

You'll find that certain plants work better in certain places.
For example, Dusty Miller works better as a filler plant away from the center.
Some plants, like roses, work best as focal plants in the center.
